Welcome

Virginia Commonwealth University is ranked one of the Top 100 Global Universities. The Global Education Office brings the world to VCU. If you are one of the growing numbers of international students and scholars at VCU — or would like to be — here you'll find a team of professionals dedicated to your success. If you're a student, alumni, staff or faculty member, or a community member hoping to increase your global connections, we can help you gain the knowledge, skills and experience increasingly needed in today's world. VCU's Global Education Office is your passport to the world.

Faculty Development Seminar

The Global Education Office is pleased to announce the inaugural Faculty Development Seminar. This new program is designed to help faculty develop and establish overseas research and teaching linkages.
