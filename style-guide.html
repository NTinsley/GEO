Images

Images take up 100% of parent container. The image below is actually only 230px wide which is why it is blurry/pixelated. Add it to col-1-* or size appropriately.

Add class="pull-left" or pull-right" to float image and constrain to 30% width. Generally, on most pages you will have text that wraps around the first page photo.
